Safety First

At Sumanglam Gas, safety isn't just a policy—it's our highest priority. Follow these guidelines for a secure home.

1. The Smell Test

If you smell gas, do not operate electrical switches. Open all windows immediately.

2. Valve Control

Always turn off the regulator valve when the stove is not in use, especially at night.

3. Proper Tubing

Use only ISI-certified Suraksha hoses and replace them every 2 years or if cracks appear.

4. Cylinder Storage

Keep cylinders upright in a well-ventilated area. Never store them in basements.
